One day, I shall come back. Yes, I shall come back. Until then, there must be no regrets, no tears, no anxieties. Just go forward in all your beliefs and prove to me that I am not mistaken in mine. Goodbye Susan. Goodbye my dear. You don't understand so you find excuses. What do you think it is, a space helmet for a cow? If you could touch the alien sand and hear the cries of strange birds, and watch them wheel in another sky, would that satisfy you? As we learn about each other, so we learn about ourselves.
